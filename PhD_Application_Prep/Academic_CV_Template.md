# Academic CV Structure

An academic CV is different from a professional resume. It focuses on research and academic achievements.

## 1. Contact Information
* **Name:**
* **Email:**
* **Phone:**
* **LinkedIn:**
* **Personal Website** (if any):

* **BS/BA in [Subject]**, [University Name], [Graduation Year]. 
  * GPA/Honors.

## 3. Research Interests
*(3-5 bullet points of specific sub-fields)*

* [Sub-field 2]
* [Sub-field 3]

## 4. Publications & Presentations
*(Any published papers, working papers, or conference presentations)*
* [Author(s), Year, Title, Journal/Conference]

## 5. Research Experience
*(Research assistant roles, data collection, methodology skills e.g., SPSS, R, Python, NVivo)*
* **[Role]**, [Institution/Project], [Dates]
  * [Key responsibilities and tools used]

## 6. Professional Experience
*(Relevant government, NGO, or policy work)*
* **[Role]**, [Organization], [Dates]
  * [Key responsibilities and achievements]

## 7. Awards & Honors
*(Any previous scholarships or academic awards)*
* [Award Name], [Granting Institution], [Year]

## 8. References
*(Contact details for 2-3 current professors who can write strong recommendation letters)*
* **[Professor Name]**, [Title], [University]
  * Email: [Email Address]
  * Relationship: [e.g., Thesis Advisor]
