# Statement of Purpose (SOP) Outline

*This is the most critical document for your actual application.*

## Paragraph 1: Introduction (The "Hook")
* What drives your passion for Public Administration? 
* State clearly that you are applying for the PhD program and mention the specific faculty member you wish to work with.

## Paragraph 2 & 3: Academic Background & MS Research
* Detail your current MS studies. 
* Explain your thesis, the methodology you used, and what you discovered. 
* Show that you know how to conduct rigorous academic research.

## Paragraph 4: Relevant Experience
* Mention any relevant work experience (NGOs, government, think tanks) and how it informs your theoretical interests.

## Paragraph 5: "Why This University & Professor?"
* This must be customized for every application. 
* Explain how your research goals perfectly match Dr. [X]'s work. 
* Mention specific labs, centers, or resources at the university.

## Paragraph 6: Future Goals & Conclusion
* Briefly state your career goals (e.g., becoming a tenure-track professor, working for an international organization).
* Reaffirm your readiness for the rigor of a PhD.
